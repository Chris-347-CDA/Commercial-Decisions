The Decision Framework

Every engagement follows the same structured thinking process.

1. Ask

What decision are we really trying to make? Everything starts with asking the right question.

2. Understand

Separate facts from assumptions. Understand what we know—and what we still need to learn.

3. Challenge

Pressure-test the thinking. Challenge assumptions. Consider alternative perspectives.

4. Explore

Evaluate realistic options. Understand trade-offs. Remove unnecessary complexity.

5. Decide

Choose the path that provides the greatest confidence—not simply the loudest opinion.

6. Learn

Capture the learning. Every important decision should improve the next one.
